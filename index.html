Complete Game Basketball Training

Welcome to Complete Game Basketball Training! We offer private individual or group basketball training sessions to boys and girls ages 10 or older, and are located in Central Massachusetts. All ability levels are welcome! Whether you want to be the best player in your town, make your school team, be a better pick-up player, or just get a workout in, we can help make you a complete basketball player. Workouts will consist of learning and improving offensive and defensive fundamentals through skill and drill work, repetition with feedback from a coach, games and challenges, and conditioning. Each lesson is tailored to meet the goals and needs of the athlete, and last for one hour. Sessions will take place at an outdoor court near you!



Coach Russell

Coach Russell is a professional teacher-coach who has coached junior varsity and middle school basketball, and has taught Physical Education for over a decade. As a husband and father to a two-year-old, he no longer has time to coach in the winter, but still loves to be involved with the game and student-athletes through training sessions. He uses similar methods in his private trainings as he does with students in his classroom. This includes goal-setting, athlete evaluation, skill assessment with feedback, progress measurement, and lesson reflection. He possesses the following qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Movement Science with a concentration in Physical Education - Westfield State University
  • Master's degree in Health, Physical Education, and Recreation - Emporia State University
  • Graduate Certificate in Athletic Administration - Southern New Hampshire University
  • Professional Development Program, Sports Psychology & Coaching - University of La Verne
